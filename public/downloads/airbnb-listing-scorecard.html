Airbnb Listing Scorecard

A 15-minute, 100-point audit for hosts before lowering price. Use this to find presentation leaks in your cover photo, first five photos, captions, copy, trust details, and competitor fit.

How to use it

  1. Open your listing and three nearby competitors at a similar price.
  2. Score each section honestly. Do not edit yet.
  3. Write one sentence explaining each low score.
  4. Pick the top five fixes before changing price.

Score interpretation

  • 80–100: likely small improvements, not a full rebuild.
  • 60–79: meaningful presentation leaks; fix before discounting.
  • Below 60: full listing order/copy/photo diagnosis is probably needed.
