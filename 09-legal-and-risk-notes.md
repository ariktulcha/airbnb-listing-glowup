# Legal and Risk Notes

This is not legal advice. These are practical risk areas to handle before scaling.

## Airbnb/platform risk

Airbnb may restrict scraping, automated browsing, commercial use of listing data, and unsolicited messaging.

Safer early approach:
- user-submitted URLs
- manual/private audit previews
- minimal stored data
- do not publicly republish scraped pages
- avoid aggressive scraping
- do not impersonate guests
- avoid spam through Airbnb messages

## Contactability risk

Finding listings is easy. Reaching hosts is hard.

Potential contact paths:
- same property on Booking.com / Vrbo / direct website
- host/property manager website
- Instagram/Facebook page
- Google Business Profile
- LinkedIn
- local STR groups
- property management company page
- paid ads targeting hosts
- partnerships with photographers, cleaners, property managers

## Copyright/photo rights

Hosts uploaded the photos. Modifying photos before being hired can be sensitive.

Safer framing:
- use watermarked preview only
- say “preview/mockup only”
- deliver full files only after approval/payment
- avoid public display without permission

## Misleading photo edits

The photo enhancement policy should prohibit:
- fake windows/views
- changing room size
- adding amenities that do not exist
- fake luxury
- unrealistic sky/view edits
- hiding material defects

Allowed direction:
- brightness
- clarity
- crop
- color balance
- perspective correction
- professional presentation while staying accurate

Positioning:

> Professionalized, not deceptive.

## Spam/trust risk

Bad framing:

> I took your photos and improved them. Pay me to unlock.

Better framing:

> I made a free watermarked preview showing how your listing presentation could be improved. If you want the full Airbnb-ready upgrade, I can deliver it for $300.

## Overpromising risk

Avoid claiming guaranteed bookings or guaranteed ranking.

Use:
- designed to improve presentation
- designed to help conversion
- may help clicks/bookings
- clearer guest positioning

Avoid:
- guaranteed first page ranking
- guaranteed revenue increase
- guaranteed occupancy uplift
