# Brand and Positioning

## Recommended positioning

Not:

> AI for Airbnb listings.

Better:

> We turn underperforming Airbnb listings into professional, high-converting listings — with better photos, stronger copy, short-form video, and a clear action plan.

## Best strategic line

> Your Airbnb may not need a better property. It may need a better presentation.

## Naming ideas

- Listing Glow-Up
- StayGlow
- HostGlow
- ListingLift
- StayBoost
- BookBetter
- Airbnb Makeover
- RentalGlow
- ListingRevamp

“Glow-Up” is strong for TikTok/Instagram because the service is visual and before/after based.

## Homepage draft

### Headline

Your Airbnb listing may be costing you bookings.

### Subheadline

We fix the photos, copy, and presentation in 48–72 hours — so your property looks more professional and gives guests a clearer reason to book.

### CTA

Get Free Listing Score

### Offer section

**Full Airbnb Listing Glow-Up — $300**

Includes:
- 15 enhanced photos
- new title
- rewritten description
- photo order
- photo captions
- short vertical video
- top 10 fixes
- competitor/pricing note

### Trust points

- Human-reviewed
- Airbnb-ready copy
- No fake or misleading edits
- Watermarked preview available
- One-time package, no subscription

## Anti-commodity positioning

Fiverr sellers optimize Airbnb copy.

We optimize the whole listing presentation:
- visuals
- copy
- video
- positioning
- competitor appeal
- conversion fixes

## Content voice

Direct, practical, visual, non-corporate.

Avoid hype. Show before/after.

## Example social hooks

- This Airbnb photo is costing the host bookings.
- This apartment is probably better than it looks.
- I would change this cover photo immediately.
- Your Airbnb title says nothing. Here is a better one.
- 3 photos I would remove from this listing.
- I turned these bad Airbnb photos into a professional listing preview.
