# Go-to-Market Plan

## Strategic approach

Build just enough credibility to sell, then validate quickly.

Do not overbuild a full SaaS or a huge brand before proving hosts pay.

## Credibility stack

### Website

One clean landing page first.

Sections:
1. Hero: Make your Airbnb listing look 10x more professional in 48 hours.
2. Before/after examples
3. What we fix: photos, title, description, photo order, video, reviews, pricing sanity check
4. Offer: Full Listing Glow-Up — $300
5. Sample deliverable
6. CTA: Send your Airbnb link / Get free listing score
7. Trust: human-reviewed, no misleading edits, Airbnb-ready copy, 48–72h delivery

### Lead magnet

Best lead magnet:

> Free Airbnb Listing Score

Input:
- Airbnb URL
- email
- city
- number of properties

Output:
- score out of 100
- 3 biggest issues
- photo score
- copy score
- CTA to buy full upgrade

This can be manual at first but presented like a tool.

### Blog

Start with high-intent posts only:

1. How to improve your Airbnb listing photos
2. Airbnb title examples that get more clicks
3. Airbnb description examples before and after
4. Why your Airbnb gets views but no bookings
5. Airbnb photo order: which photo should be first?
6. Airbnb SEO: what actually affects ranking?
7. How to make a small apartment look better on Airbnb
8. Airbnb listing checklist before publishing
9. Airbnb listing mistakes that cost bookings
10. How to optimize an Airbnb listing without hiring a property manager

### TikTok / Instagram

Best format:
- before/after listing roasts
- photo order fixes
- title rewrites
- “this Airbnb photo is costing bookings”
- “this apartment is probably better than it looks”
- AI photo/video transformations

Important: blur identifying details and avoid publicly shaming real hosts.

### LinkedIn

Useful for credibility and B2B/property manager audience.

Post about Airbnb conversion mistakes, STR revenue, AI for hospitality, before/after case studies, listing optimization frameworks, and lessons from analyzing 100 Airbnb listings.

### Outbound

The proactive sales motion:

1. find weak listing
2. score it
3. create mini preview
4. find contact route
5. send short personalized message
6. sell unlock / full upgrade

## Outreach script draft

Hey [Name], I came across your Airbnb in [City]. The property itself looks strong, but the listing presentation is probably costing you bookings — mainly the cover photo, title, and description.

I made a quick before/after preview: improved title, cleaner photo style, and a short reel mockup. You can see the watermarked preview here: [link]

If you want, I can deliver the full Airbnb-ready upgrade for $300: enhanced photos, rewritten copy, photo order, short video, and 10 concrete fixes. No subscription, one-time upgrade.

## Key warning

Do not let website/blog/social become procrastination. Build the minimum credibility layer, then start selling.
