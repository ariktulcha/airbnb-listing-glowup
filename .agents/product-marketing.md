# Product Marketing Context — Listing Glow Studio

## Category

Short-term-rental listing presentation optimization.

## One-line positioning

Listing Glow Studio helps Airbnb and short-term-rental hosts turn weak listing presentation into clearer guest trust: cover photo logic, photo order, title, description, captions, competitor context, and a paste-ready improvement file.

## ICP

Primary ICP: independent Airbnb hosts and small property managers with good-enough properties but weak listing presentation.

Secondary ICP: Airbnb photographers, co-hosts, and STR consultants who can refer or bundle the service.

## Core pain

The host is getting views, low conversion, or weak confidence and assumes the only lever is price. Often the property is not the main problem — the first impression is.

## Differentiation

Not generic AI copywriting. Not fake photo editing. Not an Airbnb ranking hack. The product is a human-reviewed listing merchandising layer.

## Proof ladder

1. Public sample audits
2. Private watermarked previews
3. Pilot host feedback
4. Real paid-client testimonials
5. Before/after case studies

## Voice

Direct, useful, ethical, practical, hospitality-aware. Avoid hype, fake guarantees, and guru language.

## Claims we can make

- clearer listing presentation
- stronger first impression
- more professional title/copy/caption/photo sequence
- better guest understanding
- paste-ready deliverable

## Claims we do not make

- guaranteed bookings
- guaranteed ranking
- guaranteed occupancy
- guaranteed revenue
- Airbnb affiliation
- fake reviews or fake amenities
