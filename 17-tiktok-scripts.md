# TikTok Content — Ryan Ashford (10 Video Scripts)

Format: short, direct, useful. 20-40 seconds. Talking-head + on-screen listing example where possible. No hype, no trending-sound gimmicks — the hook is the insight itself.

---

### 1. "Why this Airbnb cover photo is losing bookings"
**Hook:** "This Airbnb has a great apartment. This cover photo is still losing them bookings."
**Script:** Show a weak cover photo (dark, wide, empty room). "Guests decide in under a second if they'll click. This photo says 'keep scrolling.' Here's what I'd use instead—" (cut to a warmer, closer, feature-forward shot). "Same apartment. Completely different first impression."
**Visual:** Split screen weak vs. strong cover photo.
**Caption:** Your cover photo is a filter, not decoration. #airbnbtips #hostlife #shorttermrental
**CTA:** "Send me your listing and I'll tell you if your cover photo is working against you."

### 2. "3 mistakes hosts make in their listing title"
**Hook:** "If your Airbnb title says 'Cozy apartment in the city,' you're invisible."
**Script:** "Mistake 1: generic adjectives — cozy, lovely, amazing — mean nothing. Mistake 2: no specific detail — guests search for things like 'rooftop,' 'walk to beach,' 'workspace.' Mistake 3: title doesn't match who actually books this place." Show 2-3 real-style title rewrites.
**Visual:** Text overlay listing bad title → good title side by side.
**CTA:** "Want your title rewritten? That's part of the $300 glow-up."

### 3. "Your first 5 photos matter more than your description"
**Hook:** "Nobody reads your description before they judge your first 5 photos."
**Script:** Walk through the ideal 5-photo sequence: hook shot, main living/sleeping experience, kitchen/dining, bedroom quality, unique feature/view. Show a real disorganized sequence next to it.
**Visual:** Numbered photo carousel overlay 1-5.
**CTA:** "This is one part of what I fix in a full glow-up."

### 4. "Bad photo order can make a good apartment look average"
**Hook:** "This apartment is actually really nice. You wouldn't know it from the listing."
**Script:** Show the current bad order (exterior, sofa, bathroom, hallway, another sofa). "The photos are all fine individually. The order tells no story. Guests have to work to understand what they're booking — and most won't bother." Show reordered version.
**Visual:** Side-by-side sequence comparison.
**CTA:** "I fix photo order as part of every listing glow-up."

### 5. "This is what I'd fix first in a weak Airbnb listing"
**Hook:** "Give me 60 seconds with any Airbnb listing and I'll tell you the first thing to fix."
**Script:** Quick-fire teardown format — pull up a sample listing, call out: cover photo, title clarity, first caption, one description line. End with: "None of this needs a better apartment. It needs a better presentation."
**Visual:** Screen recording scroll-through with callouts.
**CTA:** "Send me yours and I'll do this for free."

### 6. "The $300 question every host should ask"
**Hook:** "Would you pay $300 to fix a listing that's costing you a booking a month?"
**Script:** Explain the math: one extra booking often covers the cost outright. Breakdown of what's included in the Glow-Up (photos, title, description, captions, video idea, top 10 list). No fake urgency — just the math.
**Visual:** Simple on-screen breakdown list.
**CTA:** "Link in bio for a free listing score first."

### 7. "Captions are doing more work than you think"
**Hook:** "This caption says nothing. Here's what I'd write instead."
**Script:** Show a blank/generic photo caption ("Living room"). Rewrite it to answer a guest doubt: "Living room with blackout curtains and a workspace — good for late check-ins or remote work." Explain: captions answer objections guests won't ask directly.
**Visual:** Before/after caption overlay on the same photo.
**CTA:** "Every photo gets a caption like this in the full package."

### 8. "Stop discounting before you check this"
**Hook:** "Before you drop your nightly rate, check if guests are even clicking your listing."
**Script:** Explain: if impressions are fine but bookings are low, it's a presentation/trust problem, not a price problem. Quick 3-question self-check guests can do themselves.
**Visual:** Simple 3-question on-screen checklist.
**CTA:** "I can score your listing for free — link in bio."

### 9. "The ethical line I won't cross"
**Hook:** "There's a version of 'AI photo enhancement' for Airbnb that I refuse to do."
**Script:** "I don't make a property look like something it isn't. I fix lighting, composition, and order — I don't add a pool that doesn't exist. The goal is guests booking what they actually get, and being happy when they arrive." Builds trust explicitly.
**Visual:** Talking head, no B-roll needed — sincerity carries this one.
**CTA:** "This is why hosts trust the process. Link in bio."

### 10. "What a $300 glow-up actually includes"
**Hook:** "Here's exactly what you get for $300 — no vague 'AI package.'"
**Script:** Quick checklist walkthrough: enhanced photos, cover recommendation, photo order, 3 title options, rewritten description, captions, short vertical video idea, competitor note, top 10 action list, paste-ready file. "48-72 hour turnaround."
**Visual:** On-screen checklist ticking off each item.
**CTA:** "Send your listing link — I'll tell you if it's a fit."

---

## Production notes
- Film all 10 in one batch against a consistent background (rental-apartment style desk/living room) to keep the feed visually coherent.
- Keep captions on-screen throughout (many viewers watch muted).
- No trending audio needed — voice + text carries the format; use light ambient/lo-fi background track only.
- Post 1 talking-head hook per video, never open with the CTA.
