# Homepage Trust/Sales Section Analysis

## Diagnosis

The previous page looked polished but not credible enough for hosts because too many visuals felt abstract/generated. For this offer, trust comes from:

1. Real-looking apartment photography.
2. Concrete mock screenshots of the audit/delivery output.
3. A visible human review layer.
4. Transparent claims: no fake edits, no ranking/revenue promises.
5. Review/social-proof area that does not pretend to have testimonials before real clients exist.

## Section-by-section decisions

### Hero

Problem: abstract generated visual felt like a concept, not a real service.

Change: replaced the hero graphic with a mock listing audit screenshot using apartment photography, score, watermark, and concrete recommendations.

Sales role: cold visitor should understand in 5 seconds: "I send my listing, they diagnose presentation problems and show a preview."

### Stats/service cards

Problem: good but generic.

Decision: keep them, because they quickly summarize deliverables.

Sales role: reduces package ambiguity.

### Photo credibility section

Problem: site needed real apartment feeling.

Change: added three photo-led cards: cover-photo choice, caption clarity, guest positioning.

Sales role: shows that the service is about listing presentation, not generic copywriting.

### Real problem section

Problem: strong copy; keep.

Sales role: reframes hosts away from lowering price first.

### Offer section

Problem: strong; keep near middle after enough context.

Sales role: clear price anchoring.

### Founder section

Problem: founder photo existed but was visually underused / sometimes appeared as an empty block in long screenshots before load.

Change: made it a stronger trust section: image card, badge, dark copy panel, "Arik · Listing Doctor" positioning, operating rule.

Sales role: makes the service feel human-reviewed, not AI spam.

### Screenshot/deliverable section

Problem: no concrete view of what the customer receives.

Change: added mock delivery screenshot with cover photo, photo order, title option, top fix.

Sales role: makes the $300 output tangible.

### Testimonials section

Problem: no real testimonials yet. Fake testimonials would hurt trust and are unethical.

Change: added transparent pilot/sample feedback area. Replace with real host name/property type as soon as first pilots are done.

Sales role: creates a proof slot now, without fabricating claims.

### Before/after section

Problem: gradient placeholders looked fake.

Change: replaced with real apartment photos and before/after framing.

Sales role: demonstrates the transformation preview concept.

## Screenshot policy

Do not use real Airbnb screenshots unless we have rights/permission and are comfortable with platform terms. Safer alternatives:

- Mock listing screenshots with stock/property photos.
- Screenshots of our own audit/report UI.
- Screenshots of delivery files/templates.
- Watermarked before/after previews made from submitted client assets only.
