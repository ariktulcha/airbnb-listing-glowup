# Upwork Profile Kit

## Profile title

Airbnb Listing Optimization Specialist | Titles, Photos, Copy & Booking Clarity

## Overview

Your Airbnb may not need a lower price. It may need a clearer first impression.

I help Airbnb and short-term-rental hosts turn underperforming listings into more professional, guest-friendly sales pages: stronger cover photo logic, better first five photos, clearer title, rewritten description, useful captions, competitor context, and a paste-ready improvement file.

This is not generic AI copywriting. The value is judgment: what the guest sees first, what feels trustworthy, what creates confusion, and what should change before you assume price is the problem.

### What I can help with

- Airbnb listing audit
- Airbnb title rewrite
- Airbnb description rewrite
- Photo order and cover photo recommendations
- Photo captions that answer guest objections
- Competitor positioning notes
- Full listing glow-up delivery file

### Best fit

- Independent Airbnb hosts
- New listings that need a stronger launch
- Good properties with weak photos/copy/order
- Small property managers who want a cleaner standard across listings

### What I do not promise

I do not guarantee Airbnb ranking, occupancy, revenue, or bookings. I improve the presentation quality and guest clarity of the listing using honest, non-misleading recommendations.

If you want, send me your listing URL and I’ll tell you the first 3 things I would fix.
