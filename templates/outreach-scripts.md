# Outreach Scripts

## Rules

- Do not impersonate a guest.
- Do not imply guaranteed bookings, ranking, occupancy, or revenue.
- Do not publicly shame the host.
- Do not say “I scraped your listing.”
- Keep the preview private and watermarked.
- Frame edits as professionalized, not deceptive.

## Version A — permission-first

Hi [Name], I came across your Airbnb in [City/Area]. The property itself looks strong, but I think the listing presentation may be underselling it — mainly the cover photo, title, and first paragraph.

I made a small private watermarked preview showing how the listing could look more professional. No fake edits and no booking guarantees — just cleaner presentation.

Want me to send it over?

## Version B — preview-first

Hi [Name], I came across your Airbnb in [City/Area]. The property looks better than the listing currently communicates, so I made a quick private watermarked preview: improved photo direction, stronger title, and a cleaner opening paragraph.

Preview: [link]

If useful, I can deliver the full Airbnb-ready Glow-Up for **$300**: enhanced photos, title/description, photo order, captions, short vertical video, competitor note, and top 10 fixes.

No subscription, no fake edits, no guaranteed-booking claims — just a one-time listing presentation upgrade.

## Version C — property manager angle

Hi [Name], I help short-term-rental hosts and small property managers make listings look more professional without changing the property itself.

I noticed one of your [City] listings has a good underlying property, but the current Airbnb presentation could be much stronger. I made a small watermarked preview showing the type of improvements I mean.

If you like the direction, the full package is **$300/listing** and includes enhanced photos, title/description, photo order, captions, a short vertical video, and a practical action list.

Can I send the preview?

## Version D — follow-up after no response

Quick follow-up — I don’t want to spam you.

The short version: your property may not be the issue; the listing presentation may be costing clicks. I made a small private preview with a stronger title/photo direction. If you want it, I’ll send it. If not, no worries.

The full Airbnb Listing Glow-Up is **$300 one-time**.

You get:

## Version F — reply to “is this AI?”

AI helps with speed, but this is not “AI copywriting.” It’s a human-reviewed listing presentation upgrade: photos, order, title, description, captions, short video, competitor note, and practical fixes.

The important part is not the tool — it’s whether the listing becomes clearer, more trustworthy, and more appealing to the right guest.
