# Partnership Outreach Scripts

## Airbnb photographer partner

Subject: Airbnb listing add-on

Hi [Name], I saw your Airbnb/real estate photography work in [City].

You already solve the biggest visual problem for hosts. I’m building a complementary listing optimization service: title, description, photo order, captions, competitor context, and a paste-ready improvement file.

The angle is simple: your photos make the property look better; we make the listing sell the stay more clearly.

Would you be open to a referral/bundle conversation? I can send a sample deliverable.

## Co-host / consultant partner

Subject: listing audits for your hosts

Hi [Name], I noticed you work with short-term-rental hosts in [Market].

I’m looking for a few co-hosts/consultants to test a simple add-on: a private listing presentation audit and paste-ready glow-up for hosts whose properties are good but listings look weaker than they should.

It covers cover photo logic, first five photos, title, captions, description, competitor notes, and top fixes.

No fake edits or booking guarantees — just clearer guest-facing presentation.

Could this be useful for any of your clients?

## Follow-up

Quick follow-up — the easiest version is that I review one listing for free and show you the deliverable. If it feels useful, we can discuss referral fee or white-label delivery for your hosts.

## Referral offer

Suggested starting partner terms:
- 15% referral fee on one-off purchases
- 20% referral fee on 3+ listing packages
- white-label delivery available after 3 successful test projects
- partner gets a free mini-audit sample for their own marketing
