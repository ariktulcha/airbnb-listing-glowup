# Apollo / Clay Prospecting Brief

Use this only after manual outreach proves the ICP and message. Do not buy or enrich a large list before reply data exists.

## Target account filters

### Property managers

- Industry keywords: short-term rental, vacation rental, property management, Airbnb management, co-hosting
- Company size: 1-50 employees
- Geography: start with Athens, Madrid, Paris, Lisbon, Rome, London, New York
- Titles: Founder, Owner, Managing Director, Operations Manager, Revenue Manager, Head of Guest Experience
- Exclude: hotels, large enterprise PMS platforms, real estate sales-only agencies

### Photographer partners

- Keywords: Airbnb photographer, real estate photographer, vacation rental photographer, interior photographer
- Company size: solo to 10 employees
- Contact: founder/owner
- Geography: same priority cities

## Buying signals

- recently posting new properties
- hiring cleaners/co-hosts/guest operations roles
- public portfolio with inconsistent listing copy
- website offers Airbnb management but no listing optimization service
- active Instagram with property photos

## Contact rules

- Use public business emails when possible.
- Keep source URL and date in CRM.
- Verify deliverability before cold email.
- Do not add private/personal emails without a lawful basis.
- Do not bulk-scrape LinkedIn or Airbnb.

## Scoring

Hot:
- 3-30 listings or clear host audience
- direct contact path
- visible listing quality gap
- likely repeat buyer/referrer

Warm:
- relevant business but weak signal
- indirect contact path
- uncertain listing volume

Skip:
- no contact route
- too large/enterprise
- irrelevant vertical
- listing/property quality issue, not presentation issue
