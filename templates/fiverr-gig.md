## FAQ

### Do you guarantee more bookings?
No. I do not guarantee ranking, occupancy, revenue, or bookings. I improve the listing presentation so guests understand the property faster and with more confidence.

### Do you edit photos?
I can recommend honest improvements like brightness, crop, straightening, order, and selection. I do not recommend misleading edits or fake amenities.

### Do I need professional photos?
No, but good source photos help. If the photos are very weak, I’ll tell you what to reshoot.

### Can you work with property managers?
Yes. Multi-listing packages are available.
