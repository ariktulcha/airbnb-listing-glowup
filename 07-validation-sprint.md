# 7-Day Validation Sprint

## Objective

Validate the business before overbuilding.

The main unknowns are not technical. AI can improve listings. The real unknowns are:

1. Can we reach hosts?
2. Do they trust us?
3. Do they pay $300?
4. Which preview format converts?
5. Which market responds best?
6. How much work is needed per delivery?
7. Does the result improve bookings?

## Success metric

At least one paid customer or three serious replies from 20 high-quality previews.

## Day-by-day plan

### Day 1 — Choose market and name

Pick one market: Athens, Tbilisi, Prague, Milan, Rome, Tel Aviv, Madeira, or Lisbon.

Choose working brand name and define the exact $300 package.

### Day 2 — Build credibility MVP

Create:
- one-page website / landing page
- one sample audit
- checkout/payment link or simple payment instruction
- CTA: Get Free Listing Score

### Day 3 — Create examples

Create 3 sample transformations:
- before/after photo examples
- title rewrite
- description rewrite
- short video/reel preview

### Day 4 — Lead list

Find 100 Airbnb listings in the chosen market.

For each, capture:
- Airbnb URL
- title
- city
- estimated ADR
- review count
- rating
- photo quality score
- description quality score
- host type
- contact found yes/no
- opportunity score

### Day 5 — Create 20 previews

For the top 20 leads:
- 3 enhanced photos with watermark
- new title
- rewritten first paragraph
- 3-bullet mini audit
- optional 15–30 sec video preview

Target time: max 10–15 minutes per prospect.

### Day 6 — Outreach

Send 20 personalized messages through legally/ethically acceptable channels.

Avoid impersonating a guest or spamming Airbnb messages.

### Day 7 — Analyze

Track sent, opened/clicked if possible, replies, price objections, paid, contactability rate, time per preview, and time per delivery.

## Lead scoring model

| Factor | Points |
|---|---:|
| Bad photos but decent property | 25 |
| Weak title/description | 20 |
| ADR above $100 | 15 |
| 20+ reviews / active listing | 15 |
| Rating above 4.6 | 10 |
| Contact info found | 10 |
| Clear competitor advantage | 5 |

Pitch only listings above 70.
